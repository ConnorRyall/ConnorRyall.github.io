About Me

Connor Ryall

Hello! I’m Connor Ryall, an English teacher from New York City with over 10 years of teaching experience. I’ve taught more than 3,000 classes to students from around the world. I specialize in making lessons fun, interactive, and tailored to your unique needs—whether you're working on general English, preparing for exams, or practicing conversation.

In addition to teaching, I’m an adventurer at heart. I love traveling, rock climbing, learning new languages, and reading. I've visited over 45 countries and speak four languages. Meeting new people and learning about different cultures are passions of mine, and I bring that enthusiasm into every lesson.

I hold degrees in finance and education (with a focus on teaching English as a second language) from universities in New York City and Madrid, Spain. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced learner, I customize each class to help you reach your goals efficiently and enjoyably.

Class Types

Students can choose a class type that best suits them. Classes are always customizable and can include more than one type if needed. Prices vary depending on the duration and class type. Classes start at $20 USD for 30 minutes. Please contact me to discuss pricing for your customized needs.

General English

Improve all aspects including speaking, reading, listening, writing, grammar, and vocabulary.

Early Learners

Introduction to English including phonics, basic vocabulary, and sentences.

Business English

Learn English for professionals, interview preparation, and role-plays.

Exam Prep

Prepare for Cambridge, Oxford, IELTS, TOEFL, and IET exams.

Conversation

Practice conversation and useful phrases. Great for students planning to live in an English-speaking country.

Science & Math

Tutoring in math, biology, chemistry, and physics.
